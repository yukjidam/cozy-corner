how to play 🥚

raising your creature: hatch an egg, then keep it happy using the buttons below the screen.

  • 🍣 feed — fills hunger
  • 🎮 play — starts the fishing minigame and fills fun
  • 💤 sleep — restores rest
  • 💊 heal — cures sickness

fishing minigame 🐟

a fish swims up and down the left-hand bar. hold down (mouse or finger) to raise the green zone, let go to let it fall.

keep the green zone over the fish to fill the catch meter. fill it 3 times before the timer runs out to win! each catch makes the next one a little faster and trickier.
